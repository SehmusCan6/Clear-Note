Cookie Policy

This website uses cookies to improve your browsing experience.

What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your browser by websites you visit. They help websites recognize you when you return.

What Types of Cookies Do We Use?

  • Essential Cookies: Necessary for the website to function properly.
  • Analytics Cookies: Help us analyze visitor data and improve our services.
  • Advertising Cookies: Used to display personalized ads based on your interests.

How Can You Manage Cookies?

You can delete or block cookies through your browser settings. However, this may affect website functionality.
